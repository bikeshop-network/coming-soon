Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Bike Shop Network ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by our B2B SaaS service.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create and manage your subscription account:

Identification and Account Information

  • Name and Email Address: Required for account creation and communication.
  • Company Information: Legal name, registered address, and SIRET (83340915400022), used for billing and legal compliance.

2. Data Hosting & Sub-processors

To provide our service, we rely on the following key third-party services that may process your data on our behalf:

  • Hosting: Vercel (USA). Provides the application hosting structure.
  • Database & Authentication: Supabase (USA). Manages our core database, user authentication, and secure file storage.
  • Payments: Paddle.com (UK/Ireland). Our Merchant of Record handles all payment transactions. **We do not store your credit card details.**
  • CRM & Communications: Zoho (EU Datacenter). Used for customer relationship management and email communications.

3. Analytics Data

To improve our platform's performance, we use Vercel Analytics and Vercel Speed Insights. These tools are selected because:

  • They collect anonymous performance and usage data.
  • They do not use cookies for tracking.
  • They do not collect any personally identifiable information.
