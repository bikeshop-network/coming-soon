Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Bike Shop Network ("we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Bike Shop Network.

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and company information.

Data Hosting & Sub-processors

To provide our service, we rely on key third-party services that may process your data on our behalf:

  • Hosting: Our application is hosted on Vercel (USA).
  • Database & Authentication: Our database, user authentication, and file storage are managed by Supabase (USA).
  • Payments: All payment information is handled directly by our Merchant of Record, Paddle.com (UK/Ireland), and we do not store your credit card details.
  • CRM & Communications: Our customer relationship management and email communications are handled by Zoho (EU Datacenter).

Analytics Data

To improve our website's performance, we use Vercel Analytics and Vercel Speed Insights. These tools collect anonymous performance and usage data, do not use cookies, and do not collect any personally identifiable information.

Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. Please contact us at privacy@bikeshop-network.com for any request.
