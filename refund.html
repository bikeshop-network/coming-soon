Refund Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

This policy outlines the conditions under which Bike Shop Network accepts refund requests for subscriptions to its B2B SaaS platform.

1. Standard Withdrawal Period

Standard Policy: Our standard policy grants all new customers a **14-day** withdrawal period from the date of purchase for any new subscription.

A full refund will be issued for any cancellation requested within this time frame.

2. Promotional Offers

Specific promotional offers, such as a launch deal, may include an extended money-back guarantee (e.g., 30 days).

The precise terms of that guarantee will be clearly stated on the offer page at the time of purchase and will supersede the standard policy for those specific transactions.

3. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund under the terms applicable to your purchase:

  1. Please contact our support team at the email address below.
  2. Include your order number or purchase details.

Refunds are processed via our Merchant of Record, **Paddle.com**. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refunded amount to appear on your statement after the refund has been confirmed by our team.
