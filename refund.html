Refund Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Promotional Offers

Specific promotional offers, such as our launch offer, may include a special money-back guarantee (e.g., 30 days). The terms of this guarantee will be clearly stated on the offer page at the time of purchase.

Standard Policy

Our standard policy grants all customers a 14-day withdrawal period from the date of purchase for any new subscription.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund under the terms of your purchase, please contact our support team at support@bikeshop-network.com with your order details. Refunds will be processed through our payment partner, Paddle.com, and may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement.
